Overview

  • Panama was the first Latin American country to adopt the US currency for legal use, apart from its own native currency, the balboa.
  • Panama is best known for the Panama Canal, but it also boasts more than 1,500 miles of pristine beach coastline.
  • Panama also has over 976 specis of birds, more than the United States and Canada combined!
  • Panama City is the only capital in the world that has a rainforest within its urban limits.

Life

Panama has a tropical climate, averaging 75°F to 86°F. Most of the country is made up of coastal lowlands. Panama is outside the hurricane belt and is generally not too affected by natural disasters, although earthquakes and floods can occur.

Culture

Spanish is the dominant language in Panama, with 14% of the population speaking English and this continues to increase. Panamaians are overwhelmingly friendly and welcoming people, and they are very warm towards foreigners.
