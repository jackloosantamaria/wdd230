Overview

Purpose

The Chamber of Commerce of Panama City is to promote the companies' freedom to develop their commercial, industrial, and professional activities. The purpose of having a website is to provide economic, commercial information and official sources. Create networking and provide advice, analysis, and interpretation of the norms that arise in public policies, regulations, and national projects.
